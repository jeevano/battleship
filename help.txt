Starting a Multiplayer Game:
    Enter a 4 digit port number between 1024 and 9999
    and click on the create a server button. Next Connect
    the two client players by using the same port number
    and clicking on the connect to server button.

Single Player Game:
    Choose the single player button to play against an AI

Rules:
    Start by placing your ships on your grid (bottom grid - you
    may also choose to rotate your ships by clicking the
    rotate button).

    Once you have placed all five ships click ready
    (wait for opponent if playing multi-player).
    Once the game begins take turns guessing the location
    of the enemy ships using the top grid. Once all enemy
    ships have been sunk or you have lost all of your ships
    the game is over. The victor is the one with surviving
    ships.
